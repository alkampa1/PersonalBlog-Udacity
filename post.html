Colombia is Often Voted One of the World’s Happiest Countries

Even throuh the chaos and unpredictability of daily life, which at times seems to run on little more than bravado and blind optimism, may have a role in Colombians’ happiness. If you don’t expect things to go right, you don’t worry so much when they go wrong. Tranquilo, relax, they’ll tell you — a refrain which can drive you into an impotent rage, or it can remind you that life’s troubles are rarely terminal.
